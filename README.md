A game project for guessing flags through multiple choices or text input.

## Diverse Flag Sets
Players can choose from three distinct categories to master:

* 🌎 **National Flags:** Every sovereign nation across the globe.

* ⚽ **World Cup**: Teams qualified for the 2026 World Cup.

* 🇧🇷 **Brazilian States:** The 26 states and the Federal District of Brazil.

## Game Modes

* **Choice (NORMAL):**
  - Classic 4-option multiple-choice format.
  - Designed for speed and pattern recognition.
  - Ideal for beginners or warming up.

* **Typing (HARD):**
  - The ultimate challenge: no hints, just a text box.
  - Multilanguage Support.
  - Timed Performance: A global timer tracks how long it takes you to name all flags in the selected set. Every second counts!

## User Experience & Social
* Google OAuth: Instant login using your Google account.

* Global Ranking: Compete for the top spot on the leaderboard.

* Personal Profile: Track your growth with personal bests, total flags guessed, and historical performance charts.
